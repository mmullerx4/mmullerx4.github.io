Article Title

Who wouldn't love to go ice skating in the beautiful outdoors with the snowy covered mountains as a backdrop? This beautiful scenery is Preston Park in Preston, Idaho. These 2 young boys prepped this ice skating rink and are very willing to share with all. The say the more the merrier. In fact they are hoping for a good turn out each evening of families sharing time together in the clean air of Preston. Tuesdays evenings are couples night, Wednesdays are ladies night, and Thursday is bring a friend night. There is also a snow hill nearby for sledding and the Preston's public pool that is fed by the local hot springs.There is Sunny's food cart available every Friday and Saturday. Some of the tasty menu items at Sunny's cart are hot chocolate, hot apple cider, hot dogs, french fries or chili fries, warm cinnamon buns and popcorn. Come and enjoy the fun!
